How to allow mods and custom content :

On Window and Macbook computers you go into your game options and go to other.
You will see the option to turn on custom content an mods aswell as script mods.

MCCC Mod by Deaderpool :

This Mod is a quick way to do the commands such as acessing the character builder, changing relationship levels, change your sims pregnancy status and easily editing your sims inventory.

Better exeptions by TwistedMexi :

This Mod checks your mods and pinpoints the problem when an error occurs within the game.

Slice of life by Kawaii Staci :

This mod adds a layer of realism into your game allowing your sim to catch and experience a wider variety of illnesses, put on make up, buy and put on perfume, buy and wear face masks and lots more.
